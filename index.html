Number Guessing Game

How to Play

  1. Enter your desired minimum and maximum numbers, then press Set Target.
  2. Type your guess into the Guess Number field.
  3. Click the Guess button to check your answer.
  4. The game will tell you if your guess was too high or too low.
  5. Adjust your guess and try again.
  6. The app will announce when you are correct and display your total number of guesses.
